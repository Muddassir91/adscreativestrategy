01

Build a research template

One doc, three sections: voice of customer (love/hate/fear), competitor teardown (hooks/angles/formats), audience patterns. Use it on three projects. Tweak it after each one.

+ Makes research repeatable
02

Start a debrief habit

After every win, write one page: what you guessed, what worked, who it worked on, what you'd try next. Do ten of these and you'll have a playbook nobody else has.

+ Makes wins compound
03

Lock in your brief format

Pick a structure: customer insight, hypothesis, hook direction, test variable. Use that same skeleton every time. The content changes. The container doesn't.

+ Makes quality consistent
